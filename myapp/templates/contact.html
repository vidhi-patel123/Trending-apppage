About the App

"Your All in one mobile App for Business, Social Media & News"

"Trendings Around" is a multi-utility mobile application that combines various features, including business listings, classified ads, job postings, and social media functionalities. It serves as a comprehensive platform for users to connect, browse services, find job opportunities, and engage socially, making it a one-stop solution for diverse community needs. It aims to be a comprehensive solution, addressing various user needs while promoting local connections. By integrating business listings, classifieds, job opportunities, and social networking, it creates a dynamic platform for users to engage with their community effectively.

Why is business listing with Trendings Around essential?

Business listings play a crucial role in enhancing a company's online presence, offering a structured avenue for businesses to increase their exposure and credibility.

They provide consistent and accurate information across various platforms, which is essential for search engine optimization and for customers seeking reliable business details.

Moreover, integrating social media within these listings can amplify a company's reach and engagement, allowing for a more dynamic interaction with the target audience. Together, business listings and social media create a synergistic effect that can significantly boost a company's visibility and customer acquisition efforts.

Having a database filled with customer information, products, and analytics alone does not generate business value.

The true value of a business database emerges when it is connected to a user base.

Application Features


Social Media

The social media feature allows users to connect, share, and engage with trending topics and events in real-time. This feature enhances user experience by promoting active participation and fostering a vibrant community focused on trending discussions.

Chat

An in-built chat feature enables real-time communication within an app or website, enhancing user engagement and providing quick access to support. It fosters community interaction and integrates with other tools for a seamless experience.

Play & Win

The "Play and Win" feature is a quiz-based engagement tool where users answer questions for a chance to earn rewards, such as cash prizes or other gifts. It promotes user interaction and competition while providing educational value.

News

The news feature is designed to keep users informed about the latest developments, trends, and events happening locally and globally. This feature enhances user engagement by providing a dynamic platform for accessing and interacting with news and community awareness.

Best Offers

The "Best Offer" feature helps businesses effectively reach out to the public by showcasing attractive promotions and discounts. It increases visibility, engages customers, provides a competitive edge, and offers insights into customer preferences.

Business Listing

This feature is designed to help users discover and connect with local businesses and services. This not only supports local businesses by increasing their visibility but also enhances user experience by providing easy access to essential services and information.

Classified Ads

The classified ads feature in the Trendings Around app provides a platform for users to buy, sell, and trade various items and services within their local community. This feature enhances community engagement by providing a convenient way for users to connect over goods and services, promoting local commerce and interaction.

Job Portal

The job portal feature serves as a comprehensive platform for job seekers and employers to connect, facilitating the recruitment process. This feature enhances the job search experience by providing a user-friendly platform for both job seekers and employers, promoting efficient hiring and career development within the community.
